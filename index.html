Features

Everything you need to track your cars

From oil changes to MOTs — every detail, every vehicle, in one place.

Multi-vehicle Garage

Track every car with make, model, year, mileage and full purchase history. Add photos and mark vehicles as sold.

Service & Repair Logs

Log maintenance and repairs with costs, dates, mileage, notes and receipt attachments. PDF and image support.

MOT & Emissions Tracking

Record test results, expiry dates and mileage. Get 45-day advance reminders before your next MOT is due.

Smart Reminders

Date-based and mileage-based reminders so nothing slips through. Mark complete when done.

To Buy List

Plan purchases with estimated costs. Convert them directly to maintenance or repair logs once completed.

Service Plans

Define reusable service intervals — e.g. oil change every 6 months / 6,000 miles — and assign them per vehicle.

10 Colour Themes

Choose from Factory, British Racing Green, Midnight Alloy, Signal Red, Petrol Blue and more. Light and dark mode included.

Regional Settings

GBP / USD / EUR, miles or kilometres, DD Mon YYYY or ISO dates. Configurable annual test labels per region.

Backup & Restore

Full ZIP export and import of your database and all attachments. Preview restore contents before committing.

Optional Auth

Disable authentication for reverse proxy setups (e.g. Authelia). bcrypt-secured when enabled.

Dashboard

Year-to-date spending, open reminders, upcoming MOTs in the next 45 days — all at a glance.

Self-hosted & Local-first

SQLite database, no external services, no tracking. Deploy with Docker in minutes. Your data stays yours.

Get Started

Up and running in minutes

Carvey runs as a single Docker container. No external database or services required.

docker-compose.yml 
services:
  carvey:
    image: ghcr.io/revise0592/carvey:latest
    ports:
      - "3000:3000"
    volumes:
      - ./data:/app/data
    environment:
      - TZ=Europe/London
    restart: unless-stopped
shell 
docker run -d \
  --name carvey \
  -p 3000:3000 \
  -v ./data:/app/data \
  -e TZ=Europe/London \
  --restart unless-stopped \
  ghcr.io/revise0592/carvey:latest
Community Applications

Carvey is available in the Unraid Community Applications store.

Open Community Applications, search for Carvey, and click Install.

Then open http://localhost:3000 in your browser. For full setup guides, environment variables and reverse proxy examples, see the README on GitHub.
